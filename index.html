How can belly buttons be used to create the next generation of plant-based meat?

Improbable Beef has synthesized plant-based beef from algae, fungi, and microorganisms, but is searching for the right bacteria that perfectly mimics the taste of beef. The company partnered with a science lab to study bacteria found in human belly buttons. Check out the sample results in the interactive information on this page. You can filter anonymous test subject numbers before comparing demographic information with the top 10 bacterial cultures, bacterial cultures per sample, and how often the participant cleans his or her belly button on a weekly basis. The next beef-flavored bacteria could be hidden in this dataset! If not, take it as a reminder to wash your belly button today.
